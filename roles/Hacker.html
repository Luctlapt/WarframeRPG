Interface (Hacker Role Ability)

A Hacker's Interface Role Ability allows them to Hack, determines how many NET Actions they can take on their Turn, and gives them access to a suite of Interface Abilities. We will be going into Hacking in-depth in the Hacking Section.

▶ Net Actions per Turn

Interface Rank 1 to 3 4 to 6 7 to 9 10
NET Actions 2 3 4 5

▶ Interface Abilities

Ability Data
Backdoor Allows the Hacker to break through Passwords and other obstructions in the Architecture.
Cloak Allows the Hacker to hide their actions in the Architecture before they leave.
Control Allows the Hacker to control things that are attached to the Architecture.
Eye-Dee Allows the Hacker to know what a found piece of data is and its value.
Pathfinder Allows the Hacker to learn the "map" of the Architecture.
Scanner Allows the Hacker to find out the locations of systems in an area.
Slide Allows the Hacker to slip away from one Black ICE that is following them.
Virus Allows the Hacker to leave a custom virus at the very core of the Architecture.
Zap A basic Hacker attack that works against both Programs and other Hackers.
