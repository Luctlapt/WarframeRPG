Reinforcement (Grineer Role Ability)

The Grineer's Role Ability is Reinforcement. Grineer can call upon the help of a group of fellow marines, based on the Grineer's Reinforcement Rank and the conditions under which the call is made. The Reinforcement will be armed and armored based on the table below, and will be played by the GM.

As a Grineer increases in Rank, they are likely to be promoted within their current military force or to be recruited by specialized corps that they can currently call Reinforcements from.

When in danger, you can call on Reinforcements from a group of your Reinforcement Rank or lower. As an Action, you attempt to roll equal or lower than your Reinforcement Rank on a d10 to get someone to respond to your call. If you abuse this, your Captain will throw you off the corps or fine you as they see fit.

After someone responds to your call, you roll a d6 to find out in how many Rounds your reinforcements will arrive on the scene. If you roll a 6 on this roll, instead of your typical reinforcement, the reinforcement that arrives will be of the next highest level of reinforcement, unless you are Rank 10, in which case two separate reinforcement groups will arrive. If nobody responds to your call, you can always try your reinforcement call again next Turn.

▶ Reinforcement Ranks ◀

Combat Number: A Skill Base used for both offense and defense. This number combines both STAT and Skill for them. You will add a d10 roll to this value whenever attacking with their carried weapons or equipment or defending. Reinforcements cannot dodge bullets.

SP: The stopping power of the armor on both their Body and Head location.

HP: The amount of Hit Points that each member of the reinforcement has.

MOVE & BODY: The reinforcement's MOVE and BODY STAT, important for movement and for some effects that reference the target's MOVE or BODY (like Death Saves).

▶ Reinforcement Rank 1 and 2

Combat Number: 8 • SP: 7 • HP: 20 • MOVE & BODY: 4

Ghouls. Four Ghouls burst from the ground onto the scene, arriving on foot. They carry Heavy Melee Weapons and wear Kevlar®.

▶ Reinforcement Rank 3 and 4

Combat Number: 10 • SP: 7 • HP: 25 • MOVE & BODY: 5

Light Units. Four light units on the prawl. They arrive in two dropships. They carry Heavy Melee Weapons and are armored in Kevlar®.

▶ Reinforcement Rank 5 thru 7

Combat Number: 14 • SP: 13 • HP: 35 • MOVE & BODY: 4

Space Marine Lancers. Two of the most basic and prevalent form of Grineer infantry. They will arrive in a Gunship, armed with Heavy Pistols and Assault Rifles. Armored in Heavy Armorjack.

▶ Reinforcement Rank 8

Combat Number: 16 • SP: 15 • HP: 50 • MOVE & BODY: 6

Space Marine Hellion. Medium armored Grineer units which are equipped with personal jet-packs that allow them to jet-jump across terrain quickly as well as hover high in the air. One arrives on a Hellion Jetpack, carrying a Very Heavy Pistol, Assault Rifle, Grenade Launcher, and wearing Flak Armor.

▶ Reinforcement Rank 9

Combat Number: 15 • SP: 18 • HP: 35 • MOVE & BODY: 4

Elite Shield Lancers Two defensive Grineer units. They carry assault rifles and rocket launchers, wearing Metalgear®. Will arrive from the air in a Crewship.

▶ Reinforcement Rank 10

Combat Number: 14 • SP: 11 • HP: 35 • MOVE & BODY: 6

Nightwatch Corps/Kuva Royal Guard Corps These are serious hitters, operating under the control of the queens themselves. They travel in pairs, will arrive in a Crewship, and are outfitted with Very Heavy Pistols, Assault Rifles, in Light Armorjack.

Unlike all other forms of Reinforcements, these serious hitters stick around after the conflict ends and assist in investigating the scene. While they will not travel with the Crew on a day-to-day basis, after the first time they are called the same 2 serious hitters will always respond to calls for reinforcement connected to the initial call until the "case" is closed, or they fall in the line of duty.

Additionally, they can use their Combat Number for these Skills: Accounting, Acting, Conceal/Reveal Object, Criminology, Cryptography, Deduction, Education, Forgery, Interrogation, Paramedic, Perception, Personal Grooming, Resist Torture/Drugs, Stealth, and Tracking.
