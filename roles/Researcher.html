Medicine (Researcher Role Ability)

Researchers keep people alive who should be dead with their knowledge and training. In the vastness of the Origin system, they are as much mechanics as they are doctors, caring for people who are oftentimes more machine than human. The Researcher Role Ability is Medicine. Whenever the Researcher increases their Medicine Rank, they also choose one of the following three Medicine Specialties (Surgery, Biotech [Pharmaceuticals], or Biotech [Cryopod Operation]) to allocate 1 point to.

The Medical Section contains additional uses for both the Surgery and Biotech Skills, which are only available to Researchers through this Role Ability (See pg. 226). These include Modification installation and harvesting, Bodysculpting, and Therapy!

▶ Surgery

For every point you allocate to Surgery, you gain 2 points in the Surgery Skill (up to a maximum of 10). The Surgery Skill is the TECH Skill used to treat the most severe Critical Injuries, as well as implant modifications, and is only available to Researchers through this Medicine Specialty.

▶ Biotech (Pharmaceuticals)

For every point you allocate to Biotech (Pharmaceuticals), you gain 1 point in the Biotech Skill (up to a maximum of 10). The Biotech Skill is the TECH Skill used to operate, understand, and repair (as other non-vehicle Tech Skills) medical machinery. This Skill is only available to Researchers, and only through either this Medicine Specialty or Cryopod Operation. You can only put a maximum of 5 points into this specialty.

Each time you allocate a point into Pharmaceuticals you also gain access to one of the following pharmaceuticals, which your Character can synthesize by rolling a DV13 Biotech Check, wasting the materials used on a failure. A Researcher can make a number of doses from 200eb of materials equal to their Biotech Skill in 1 hour. You can't synthesize Street Drugs with Biotech (Pharmaceuticals).

Pharmaceutical Effect
Antibiotic When injected with a dose of Antibiotic, a target who has already started the natural healing process heals an extra 2 Hit Points every day for a week. A person can only benefit from one use of Antibiotic at a time.
Rapidetox When injected with a dose of Rapidetox, a target who is affected by a drug, poison, or intoxicant is immediately purged of the effects of that substance.
Speedheal When injected with a dose of Speedheal, a target who is not in the Mortally Wounded Wound State immediately heals an amount of HP equal to their BODY + WILL. A person can only benefit from one use of Speedheal per day.
Stim When injected with a dose of Stim, a target can ignore all penalties from being in the Seriously Wounded Wound State for an hour. A person can only benefit from one use of Stim per day.
Surge When injected with a dose of Surge, a target can function unimpaired without sleep for a full 24 hours. A person can only benefit from one use of Surge per week.

Applying a single dose of a drug to a target takes an Action. If the target is unwilling, the Medtech can use their Action to attempt to make a single Melee Weapon Attack with their Airhypo against the target, that administers a single dose to their target on a hit instead of dealing damage.

A Character who is not a Medtech cannot administer Pharmaceuticals correctly. These aren't Street Drugs, they require training to get the medically correct proportions.

▶ Biotech (Cryopod Operation)

For every point you allocate to Biotech (Cryopod Operation), you gain 1 point in the Biotech Skill (up to a maximum of 10). The Biotech Skill is the TECH Skill used to operate, understand, and repair (as other non-vehicle Tech Skills) medical machinery. This Skill is only available to Medtechs, and only through either this Medicine Specialty or Pharmaceuticals. You can only put a maximum of 5 points into this specialty.

When you allocate points into Cryopod Operation you also gain a benefit as detailed below:

Level Benefit
1 With 1 point in Cryopod Operation you gain one Cryopump.
2 With 2 points in Cryopod Operation you become a Registered Cryopod Technician and gain unlimited 24/7 access to 1 Cryopod at a time at any Cryopod facility operated by medical corporations or government agencies.
3 With 3 points in Cryopod Operation you gain 1 Cryopod, installed in a room of your choosing.
4 With 4 points in Cryopod Operation you gain 2 more Cryopods that can fit in the same room as your first one and your Cryopump has 2 charges and its maximum carrying capacity increases to 2 people in stasis.
5 With 5 points in Cryopod Operation you gain 3 more Cryopods that can fit in the same room as the first three and your Cryopump has 3 charges and its maximum carrying capacity increases to three people in stasis.
