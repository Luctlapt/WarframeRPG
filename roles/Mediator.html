Standing (Mediator Role Ability)

The Mediator's Role Ability is Standing. Mediators know how to get things on the black market and are adept at navigating the complex social customs of The Hubs, where hundreds of cultures and economic levels collide. Mediators maintain vast webs of contacts and clients.

Contacts represents who the Mediator can reach out in order to source goods, favors, or information. The Mediator will still have to pay for these, of course.

Reach is the highest price category of items that a Mediator can always source, and if they can use their influence to gather other Mediators into creating a Relay, which makes all price categories of items available to them for a short time. (See Relay for rules on setting up Relays)

Haggle is the ability of the Mediator to strike a deal. When haggling with a person, you roll COOL + Trading Skill + Your Standing Rank + 1d10 against their COOL + Trading Skill + Their Standing Rank (if they are a Mediator) + 1d10. If you succeed, you can make 1 deal of your Standing Rank or lower. Only 1 Mediator deal can be made per transaction.

Grease represents the Mediator's ability to blend into the many cultures on and off The Hubs; ability to know the language, social codes, and status marks for each group or culture.

▶ Standing Ranks ◀

▶ Standing Ranks 1 and 2

Contacts & Clients: Local honcho, gang lord, local planet leadership

Reach: You can always find a place to source Cheap and Everyday items for your clients on a piece by piece basis, even if they are otherwise unavailable.

Haggle: If successful, you can get 10% more or less than market price when buying or selling.

Grease You know the cultural ins-and-outs of your immediate neighborhood including all local gangs.

▶ Standing Ranks 3 and 4

Contacts & Clients: Grineer honcho, minor politician, Corpus Exec, well known person in the neighborhood.

Reach: You can always find a place to source up to Expensive items for your clients on a piece by piece basis, even if they are otherwise unavailable.

Haggle: If successful, when you buy 5 or more of the same item, you can get one more of that item for free.

Grease You know how to get along well with at least 1 other culture in the Origin system as well as gaining a single language you don't already know associated with that culture at Skill Level 4.

▶ Standing Ranks 5 and 6

Contacts & Clients: Major Grineer player, Corpus politico, neighborhood celebrity.

Reach: Once per month, working with other Mediators of your rank, you can set up a Relay. While at a Relay that you have helped organize, you can always find a place to source up to Super Luxury items.

Haggle: If successful, you can negotiate the pay per person for a Job up 20%.

Grease You know how to get along perfectly with 2 additional cultures (3 in total) in the Origin System as well as gaining a single language which you don't already know associated with each culture at Skill Level 4.

▶ Standing Ranks 7 and 8

Contacts & Clients: Local Corpus president, Captain or Grineer manager, local celebrity.

Reach: You can always find a place to source up to Very Expensive items for your clients on a piece by piece basis, even if they are otherwise unavailable.

Haggle: If successful, when buying a Luxury or Super Luxury item, you can pay half now and half in one month. If you ever don't pay the second half on time, nobody will do this deal with you again.

Grease You know how to blend in perfectly with 3 additional cultures (6 in total) in the Origin System as well as a gaining single language which you don't already know associated with each culture at Skill Level 4.

▶ Standing Ranks 9

Contacts & Clients: Divisional Corpus head, state or planet Grineer politico, well known celebrity.

Reach: You can always find a place to source up to Luxury items for your clients on a piece by piece basis, even if they are otherwise unavailable. When you set up a Relay, you can choose to additionally set up a Syndicate Hub inside it, which gathers the leadership of the all syndicates.

Haggle: If successful, you can get 20% more or less than market price when buying or selling.

Grease You know how to blend in perfectly with not only many cultures in your area, but also with Corpus and Grineer agencies.

▶ Standing Ranks 10

Contacts & Clients: Major grineer leader, major Corpus head, world-famous celebrity.

Reach: You can always find a place to source up to Super Luxury items for your clients on a piece by piece basis, even if they are otherwise unavailable.

Haggle: If successful, you can negotiate to double the pay per person for a Dangerous Job.

Grease You can blend in seamlessly with almost any group, including very specialized or "tight" groups such as secret societies, cults, or exclusive membership groups.
