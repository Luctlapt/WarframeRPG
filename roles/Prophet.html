Charismatic Impact (Prophet Role Ability)

The Prophet has the Role ability Charismatic Impact. They can influence others by sheer presence of personality. They need not be a religious figure ; they can influence others through poetry, art, dance, or simply their physical presence. They could be a prophet—or a musical performer.

A Prophet can only use their Charismatic Impact Role Ability on Followers.

Assuming you aren't in combat, you can make people who aren't currently followers into followers (unless they actively dislike you) by rolling Charismatic Impact + 1d10 vs. a DV8 for a Single Person, DV10 for a Small Group of up to 6, or DV12 for a Huge Group.

The GM determines whenever someone you meet is already a Follower.

When a Prophet wants to make use of their Charismatic Impact on a follower or group of followers, the GM uses the table below to determine if the favor is something within the powers of their Charismatic Impact given their current Role Ability Rank. If it isn't, the Prophet automatically fails. If it is, the group size determines the DV against which the Prophet must roll Charismatic Impact + 1d10. If they succeed, the follower or group of followers puts their best effort toward the favor the Prophet asked for. If they fail, the Prophet can't ask for the same favor again from those followers for a week.

▶ Charismatic Impact Ranks ◀

Venues You Can Play: The best type of venue your Prophet can hope to play under most circumstances.

Impact on a Single Follower (DV8): The impact your Prophet can have on a single follower by beating a DV8 on their Charismatic Impact Check.

Impact on a Small Group of Followers (DV10): The impact your Prophet can have on a group of up to six followers by beating a DV10 on their Charismatic Impact Check.

Impact on a Huge Group of Followers (DV12): The impact your Prophet can have on a large group of followers gathered to see them by beating a DV12 on their Charismatic Impact Check.

▶ Charismatic Impact Ranks 1 and 2

Venues You Can Play: Small local churches

Impact on a Single Follower (DV8): Prophet can convince fan to do a small favor for the Prophet; buy the Prophet a drink or meal, give them a lift somewhere.

Impact on a Small Group of Followers (DV10): Prophet has a group of up to 6 followers to ask for autographs and other personal totems; followers will stop Prophet in streets to befriend them.

Impact on a Huge Group of Followers (DV12): You're kidding, right? You don't have huge groups of followers yet.

▶ Charismatic Impact Ranks 3 and 4

Venues You Can Play: Well known churches

Impact on a Single Follower (DV8): Prophet can convince follower to do a major favor for the Prophet; go to bed with the Prophet, put a good word in for them, etc.

Impact on a Small Group of Followers (DV10): Convince a group of up to 6 followers to regularly hang out with Prophet; provide booze, drugs, or other party favors to the Prophet.

Impact on a Huge Group of Followers (DV12): Prophet has a strong local following; followers buy their recordings and merch.

▶ Charismatic Impact Ranks 5 and 6

Venues You Can Play: Large, important churches

Impact on a Single Follower (DV8): Prophet can convince follower to commit a minor crime for Prophet; shoplift, help out in a fight.

Impact on a Small Group of Followers (DV10): Convince a group of up to 6 followers to act as the Prophet's personal "posse"; constantly hang out with them, do Prophet favors, and provide things for their personal needs.

Impact on a Huge Group of Followers (DV12): Prophet's followers are all over the Planet, often in nearby planets. They are strongly loyal and will often do major favors for the Prophet in exchange for attention.

▶ Charismatic Impact Ranks 7 and 8

Venues You Can Play: Small concert halls, local video feed

Impact on a Single Follower (DV8): Follower is willing to risk their life for Prophet without question.

Impact on a Small Group of Followers (DV10): Convince a group of up to 6 followers to commit a minor crime for Prophet; shoplift, help in a fight.

Impact on a Huge Group of Followers (DV12): The Prophet's followers are rabidly loyal. They fight with rival follower groups, support strong follower information networks, will band together to help Prophet.

▶ Charismatic Impact Rank 9

Venues You Can Play: Large concert halls, national video feed

Impact on a Single Follower (DV8): Prophet can convince follower to commit major crime for Prophet; steal expensive item, beat someone up for Prophet.

Impact on a Small Group of Followers (DV10): Convince a group of up to 6 followers to commit a major crime for Prophet; steal expensive item, beat someone up.

Impact on a Huge Group of Followers (DV12): The Prophet's followers are basically a brainwashed, cult-like following; they will riot, destroy property, and even kill for the Prophet.

▶ Charismatic Impact Rank 10

Venues You Can Play: Huge stadiums or international video

Impact on a Single Follower (DV8): Follower is willing to sacrifice self for Prophet without question.

Impact on a Small Group of Followers (DV10): Convince a group of up to 6 followers to risk their lives for the Prophet; to act as personal protection.

Impact on a Huge Group of Followers (DV12): The Prophet's followers are now a worldwide following with strong, cult-like attributes. They will do almost anything for the Prophet if asked; they are a private army based on the Prophet's charisma.
