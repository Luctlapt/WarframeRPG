Factions

Corpus

"Ours is the grasping golden hand. We are desire. We are Corpus."

Headquarters: Corposium

Planetary Claims: Europa, Jupiter, Neptune, Phobos, Pluto, Venus

Chairman of the Board: Frohd Bek

Immortal Unit: Sister of Parvos

▶ Background ◀

The Corpus embody the cold vacuum of space - heartless and bent on taking anything left unprotected. Driven by profits, the Corpus Board’s small numbers are supplemented by their legion of robot proxies, optimized for quick and efficient destruction.

The Corpus are a merchant cult, built on the foundation of salvaged technology and robotics. They scavenge the outer systems, greedy for the Old War salvage. Their most sought after of all - the Tenno and their WARFRAME armor. Those captured are treated as salvage; meticulously dissected, integrated. The remains are sold to the highest bidder.

The Board of Directors is the leadership council of the Corpus, composed of the faction's various leaders. Little is known about the members or their roles. The Board's current Chairman is Frohd Bek.

The Corpus Guilds are companies and corporations ran by several Corpus merchants and the Corpus Board of Directors. Their symbols, emblems and writing can be seen printed, stamped and displayed in many locations of Corpus Locations. Some of them are:

Luxor Nanocomposites Foundry Forge: Luxor (Luksor NanokomPosites Foundry Forge, tl. Luksor Nanokomposites Foundry Forge) is the prime manufacturer of Corpus weaponry, equipment and construction materials. Their logo can be found stamped on equipment such as Crewman helmets, on weapons such as the Dera, is displayed on holographic signs in Fortuna and in Corpus Gas Cities and is printed on many surfaces of facilities in most Corpus Locations. They are represented by a circle within a triangle within a circle.

Utilizing their Nanocomposite Foundry Forges, whose technology might be related to Tenno Foundries, Luxor produces the vast majority of Corpus technology on a massive scale. As such, it is likely owned by the Corpus Board of Directors as seen by their prevalence and iconography present alongside Alad V and Nef Anyo in displays found in Corpus Gas Cities and Fortuna, respectively. Perrin Sequence crewmembers may be referencing Luxor foundry forges when they talk about "machines that can make other machines."

Beecloud Hivemind: Beecloud (Beekloud Hivemind, tl. Beekloud Hivemind) is the company responsible for the artificial intelligence of Corpus Proxies. Represented by a beehive, Beecloud created and regulates the near-sentient artificial intelligences of Proxies. It is possible that Beecloud is owned by Alad V, as the logo is both present on his collar and on barrels of volatile and other substances produced by Alad's Gas Cities.

Chiltot Hivemind Manufacturing: Chiltot (Chiltot Hivemind Manufakturing, tl. Chiltot Hivemind Manufakturing) is a minor producer of vault-like doors present in Corpus Outpost and Corpus Ice Planet Tile Sets. They are represented by a pair of interlocked keys, with Chilton Hivemind MFG mirrored on both doors in Leonardo Da Vinci style. Note the Luxor logo also appears on those doors, making Chiltot's true activities unknown.

Fortunate Dawn Security Systems: Fortunate Dawn (Fortunate Dawn Sekurity Systems, tl. Fortunate Dawn Sekurity Systems) is responsible for the development of software and hardware of Corpus security systems. Their logo is represented as a 5-pointed star and can be seen on Corpus Control Consoles and on Corpus Datamass. Text seen on security terminals may indicate that Fortunate Dawn is a subsidiary of Beecloud.

OKLU Technical, Inc: OKLU (OKLU Teknikal Ink, tl. OKLU Teknikal Ink) is a database management company which analyzes and catalogues information used in Corpus systems. Their sigil is four letters set in a diamond shape surrounded by two intertwined snakes, and is present on holographic displays, the exterior of Pillar Corpus Ships in Corpus Proximas in Empyrean missions and near the Fortunate Dawn logo on Corpus Outpost and Corpus Ice Planet landing pads.

Anyo Corp.: Anyo Corp. (Anyo Korp, tl. Anyo Korp.) is the corporation owned by Corpus magnate Nef Anyo and represents all of his scientific developments and ownings. i>Anyo Corp. is behind the creation of Razorbacks, Raknoid technology, Bursas, Hyenas, Raptors, along with the revival of the Orb Vallis. The i>Anyo Corp. logo is a Corpus letter "A" within a circle and is stamped on every tile of Nef Anyo’s robe.

Additonally, Anyo Corp. is made of numerous sub-divisions that aid in the corporation's function and business, such as; Robotics Technology Division, Feed and Research Division, Loan Reclamation Division, Claims Investigation, the Order of Profit, and Investor Relations department.

Some possible divisions may include the Trading Group and Zenith Galatical.

Index Brokers: The Brokers are members of Anyo Corp. that are employed in The Index (The Indeks, tl. The Indeks), a gambling activity that is organized by Nef Anyo, to face off against any and all rival investors for the sake of their own lives. The broker divisions are all represented under a stylized logo, possibly referring to a crewman's suit.

▶ Face: Parvos Granum ◀

Parvos Granum is the original founder of the Orokin-era Corpus merchant cult and the inventor of Specter technology. As part of a deal he made with the Orokin, he received the ProteaIcon272 Protea Warframe as a bodyguard. He was presumed dead for millennia after being betrayed by the Corpus Board of Directors, where an assassination attempt resulted in his ship being lost during a faulty Void translation.

Unlike the current-era Corpus, most notably Nef Anyo, who have achieved their wealth through slothful means, Parvos Granum is a cold, calculating, and intelligent individual who values hard work and will actively take steps and measures to achieve his goals. Disappointed in how low the Corpus have fallen on his return from the Void, Parvos proclaims he will restructure the Corpus or will otherwise separate himself from them to form a splinter faction. Following his return, the current Corpus Board of Directors have been largely absent as Parvos leads Corpus forces in space, formed the Sisters of Parvos with Vala Glarios as his assistant, has dispatched salvage teams to the Zariman Ten Zero, and hired Drusus to acquire Entrati artifacts from Albrecht's Laboratories.

The Board of Directors is the leadership council of the Corpus, composed of the faction's various leaders. Little is known about the members or their roles. The Board's current Chairman is Frohd Bek.

The Corpus Guilds are companies and corporations ran by several Corpus merchants and the Corpus Board of Directors. Their symbols, emblems and writing can be seen printed, stamped and displayed in many locations of Corpus Locations. Some of them are:

Luxor Nanocomposites Foundry Forge: Luxor (Luksor NanokomPosites Foundry Forge, tl. Luksor Nanokomposites Foundry Forge) is the prime manufacturer of Corpus weaponry, equipment and construction materials. Their logo can be found stamped on equipment such as Crewman helmets, on weapons such as the Dera, is displayed on holographic signs in Fortuna and in Corpus Gas Cities and is printed on many surfaces of facilities in most Corpus Locations. They are represented by a circle within a triangle within a circle.

Utilizing their Nanocomposite Foundry Forges, whose technology might be related to Tenno Foundries, Luxor produces the vast majority of Corpus technology on a massive scale. As such, it is likely owned by the Corpus Board of Directors as seen by their prevalence and iconography present alongside Alad V and Nef Anyo in displays found in Corpus Gas Cities and Fortuna, respectively. Perrin Sequence crewmembers may be referencing Luxor foundry forges when they talk about "machines that can make other machines."

Beecloud Hivemind: Beecloud (Beekloud Hivemind, tl. Beekloud Hivemind) is the company responsible for the artificial intelligence of Corpus Proxies. Represented by a beehive, Beecloud created and regulates the near-sentient artificial intelligences of Proxies. It is possible that Beecloud is owned by Alad V, as the logo is both present on his collar and on barrels of volatile and other substances produced by Alad's Gas Cities.

Chiltot Hivemind Manufacturing: Chiltot (Chiltot Hivemind Manufakturing, tl. Chiltot Hivemind Manufakturing) is a minor producer of vault-like doors present in Corpus Outpost and Corpus Ice Planet Tile Sets. They are represented by a pair of interlocked keys, with Chilton Hivemind MFG mirrored on both doors in Leonardo Da Vinci style. Note the Luxor logo also appears on those doors, making Chiltot's true activities unknown.

Fortunate Dawn Sekurity Systems) is responsible for the development of software and hardware of Corpus security systems. Their logo is represented as a 5-pointed star and can be seen on Corpus Control Consoles and on Corpus Datamass. Text seen on security terminals may indicate that Fortunate Dawn is a subsidiary of Beecloud.

▶ Face: Parvos Granum ◀

Parvos Granum is the original founder of the Orokin-era Corpus merchant cult and the inventor of Specter technology. As part of a deal he made with the Orokin, he received the ProteaIcon272 Protea Warframe as a bodyguard. He was presumed dead for millennia after being betrayed by the Corpus Board of Directors, where an assassination attempt resulted in his ship being lost during a faulty Void translation.

Unlike the current-era Corpus, most notably Nef Anyo, who have achieved their wealth through slothful means, Parvos Granum is a cold, calculating, and intelligent individual who values hard work and will actively take steps and measures to achieve his goals. Disappointed in how low the Corpus have fallen on his return from the Void, Parvos proclaims he will restructure the Corpus or will otherwise separate himself from them to form a splinter faction. Following his return, the current Corpus Board of Directors have been largely absent as Parvos leads Corpus forces in space, formed the Sisters of Parvos with Vala Glarios as his assistant, has dispatched salvage teams to the Zariman Ten Zero, and hired Drusus to acquire Entrati artifacts from Albrecht's Laboratories.
